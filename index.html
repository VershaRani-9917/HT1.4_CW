Indian Cuisine

Biryani

Biryani is a fragrant and aromatic rice dish with various reginal variations. Notable ingredients include:

  1. Basmati rice
  2. Chicken or lamb (or vegetables for a vegetarian version)
  3. Spices (such as cumin,coriender, and gram masala)
  4. Yogurt and saffron for-flavor and color
